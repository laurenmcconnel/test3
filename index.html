Biography

Hi, I'm Lauren McConnell

a Final Year International Business Student with Japanese and Business Analytics

With a background in business and languages, my journey with Business Analytics began only this year. However, it has sparked my interest. One of my goals is to introduce more women to the interesting facets of Business Analytics!

I gained an interest in business analytics through a desire to learn coding. It was during my year abroad in Japan that I developed an interest in analytics. So, I began research into Python in my free time. I had done some courses in business analytics in my first few years in DCU but it had revolved around learning Tableau and Excel. In my final year I was given the opportunity to specialise in Business Analytics, which involved us learning Python and SQL among other things and I jumped at the opportunity. I look forward to gaining valuable insights from data and using my diverse experiences to improve decision making.
